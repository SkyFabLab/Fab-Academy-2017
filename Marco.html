Me

Hi, my name is Marco Wehrfritz. I am a Physicist and I just moved from Germany to the Bay Area in California. I am the responsible Lab Technician for Fab Lab at the Skyline College.
This is the first Fab Lab in a Community College and we are just getting started! I am tinkering and prototyping all my life. As a kid, I made lots of model planes, during my vocational education, I built laser and as a student, I worked at a particle accelerator, where I build prototypes for detectors. After that, I was working as research associate in a consulting company for nuclear safety. After quitting my desk job and moving to the US, I used the opportunity to work in a Lab again. Teaching and Tinkering!
I don’t know yet exactly, what I am going to build as my final project. My friend and colleague Nick Langhoff and I melting our brains and see what will be the outcome. First of all, I am very excited and curious, to learn about the possibilities of the Fab Lab.

I am thinking, this page would be soemthing like a living document...of course! It will be improved and updated, as I proceed in the training.
